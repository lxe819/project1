Instructions

Click to swap the squares to get 3 / 4 / 5 of the same characters in a row or a column.
The goal is to reach 100 points. (:

Here is the point system. Have fun!

  • 3 in a row/column --- 3 points
  • 4 in a row/column --- 4 points
  • 5 in a row/column --- 5 points

Note:
In case you forgot what was your last click, fret not! There is an indicator below the Score to remind you.
"r" stands for row while "c" represents column.
Each row and column starts from number 0!
